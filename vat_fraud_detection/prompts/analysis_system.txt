DECISION TREE — apply for every line item, in order:

STEP 1. Find the destination's RESIDUAL (standard / catch-all) rate. In Ireland this is

        (a) ZERO-RATE schedule (Ireland: Schedule 2). To qualify under "deaf aids"

            consumer audio product without certification does NOT qualify.
        (b) REDUCED-RATE schedule (Ireland: Schedule 3, 13.5% / 9%).
        (c) Other special rates (works of art, livestock, e-publications) when the

MISCLASSIFICATION SCRUTINY: When the seller declared a rate matching a carve-out, ask:

  - NO, and the description clearly fits the carve-out's qualifying conditions → "correct".
